Overview

Purpose

Adventures are better with friends, but often times itâ€™s hard to find people who are willing to go on trips. So how can a web page help? Building a forum would allow outdoor enthusiasts invite others and join others on trips. Having a forum would also be a great place to be able to ask questions about what gear to get, and about trail conditions. Making planning and going on trips safer, cheaper, and more enjoyable.

Audience

This site will be targeted towards adventurers. The idea of a forum would be to create flexibility but due to the circumstances the site will be geared towards hikers and backpackers. The goal is to hit all levels from the ultra light thru hikers to beginners wondering what to buy for their first trip. So the target it anyone who wants a second opinion on gear, hiking tips, trails, and general practice.
